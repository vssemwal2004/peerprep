FAQsQuick answers about PeerPrep

Everything you need to know about mock interviews, scheduling, feedback, assessments, and analytics.

What is PeerPrep?
PeerPrep is a placement preparation platform that combines mock interviews, DSA practice, assessments, and performance analytics into one focused workflow.
How do mock interviews work?
You join an interview event, get paired automatically, propose a suitable time, confirm a slot with your partner, and join the session using the generated meeting link.
When will the meeting link be available?
After the interview is scheduled, a meeting link is generated. The UI typically allows joining closer to the interview time to keep sessions organized.
Can I reschedule if the proposed time doesn’t work?
Yes. You and your partner can propose a new time and confirm it. The scheduling flow keeps the latest proposal active and records past proposals for clarity.
Who can submit feedback and when?
Feedback is submitted after the session by the interviewer. This keeps feedback consistent and aligned with the completed interview.
What do assessments include?
Assessments are structured practice tests with clear rules and a timed environment. Your submissions and outcomes are tracked so you can review progress.
How does Problem Solve help my preparation?
It helps you practice curated DSA problems with a clean workflow for revision, pattern-building, and steady improvement.
What can I see in Analyse Performance?
You can view trends and gaps across practice and sessions to prioritize what to improve next, instead of relying on guesswork.
Do I need to install anything to use PeerPrep?
No. PeerPrep runs in the browser. You just need an internet connection and a modern browser.
Is my data private?
PeerPrep is designed to handle your account and preparation data responsibly. You can review the Privacy Policy for full details.
